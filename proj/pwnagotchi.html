Pwnagotchi (and Bjorn)

You can do a ton of stuff with microcomputers. In cybersecurity, you will end up with an endless list of projects. All you need is the luxury of having the time to dive into most of them. After gaining some hands-on experience with Raspberry Pi Zero 2W (02w) during one of my internships, I have decided to configure my own. What is a Raspberry Pi?

A Raspberry Pi is a low-cost, small/compact, single-board computer (SBC) produced by the Raspberry Pi Foundation. They were mainly established to promote computing education. Essentially, they function as a complete computer (without the peripherals) and widely used for DIY projects.
